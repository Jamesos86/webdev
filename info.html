Information

The village of Sneem was said to be established as far back as 1750 but wasnt developed until around 1837.

Sneem is famous for its position on the Ring of Kerry, sometimes being reffered to as the "Knot" of the ring.

Despite having a relitively small population of less than 500 people, Sneem receives thousands of toursists per year.

The village is split into two halves, seperated by a bridge over the Sneem river.
