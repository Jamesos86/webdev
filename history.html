History

Important Dates

  1. The village is estimated to have been established around 1750
  2. The Sneem Bridge was built around 1870-1890
  3. In 1969 Former President of France Charles de Gaulle visited the village
  4. The 'Peaceful Panda' statue was donated bu the peoples republic of China in 1986
  5. Sneem won a gold medal in the Tidy Towns in 1987 and 2016
  6. In the year 2000 a time capsule was buried in the middle of the town to be opened in 2100
