Information

The village of Sneem was said to be established as far back as 1750 but wasnt developed until around 1837.

Sneem is famous for its position on the Ring of Kerry, sometimes being reffered to as the "Knot" of the ring.

Over the years, the competition has grown into one of the most prestigious rugby tournaments in the world, watched by millions of fans annually.

The championship introduced bonus points in 2017 to encourage attacking play and make the competition more exciting.
